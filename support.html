Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Fixla app from the App Store and tap "Sign Up" to create your account.

How do I book a service?

Open the app, select the service you need, provide details about your request, and confirm your booking.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna through our secure payment system.

How do I become a service provider?

Sign up as a worker in the app and complete the application process. Our team will review your application and notify you once approved.

App Issues

If you're experiencing technical issues with the app, please include the following in your email:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots if applicable
